# Vampirism

## Feed. Hunt. Ascend.

## Before the first hunt

- **How infection starts:** the curse can begin after a vampiric kill or by drinking the transformation potion. Infection is temporary. It gives you a short window to finish the change.
- **How to become a real vampire:** while infected, use **Blood Sucker** and finish a victim before the infection timer expires. If you fail, the temporary vampiric state fades and you have to start again.
- **How the relic works:** after ascension, your **Vampirism Relic** becomes your skill focus. It has five configurable slots — **primary** (`Left Click`), **secondary** (`Right Click`), **ability1** (`Q`), **ability2** (`E`), and **ability3** (`R`) — and targeted skills fire at whatever entity you are aiming at.
- **How to rebind abilities:** crouch (`Ctrl`) and use (`F`) the relic to open the bindings menu, or open it directly with `/relicbindings` or `/relicbinding`.
- **If the relic goes missing:** free a visible inventory slot and use `/vampirismrelic get` to pull it back.

**Vampirism** turns Hytale into a darker survival-power fantasy built around infection, hunger, blood, and the pull of the night. You do not become a vampire instantly. First comes the curse. Then the struggle to survive it. Then the first real hunt. From that point on, the game shifts: blood becomes your fuel, sunlight becomes a threat, and the night starts to feel like home.

This mod is built around progression. Feed to stay strong, spend blood to unleash abilities, unlock passives through the skill tree, and shape your own playstyle as your powers grow. Chase sustain, mobility, aggression, utility, or full transformation. Use **Blood Sucker** to finish weakened prey, convert health into blood when you are desperate, slip across the world in **Bat Form**, or lean into heavier power through **Ancient Form**.

The world pushes back. Daylight is not cosmetic here. **Sunburn** builds pressure fast and punishes bad timing, bad positioning, and overconfidence. At night, the balance flips. **Night Hunts** send you after blood omens, trails, waypoints, and marked prey, turning the map into part of the hunt and making every successful kill feel earned.

Everything is supported by a stronger visual identity: custom HUD, status icons, screen effects, hunt markers, and feedback designed to make the whole experience feel sharp, predatory, and alive.

Vampirism is not just a pack of vampire abilities. It is a full gameplay loop built around transformation, pressure, and power — where every night makes you stronger, and every sunrise reminds you what you have become.

## Systems

**Infection and ascension**
The transformation starts before the power. Players can be infected, enter a temporary cursed state, and only complete the change through feeding and successful hunts.

**Blood economy**
Blood is more than a bar. It is the resource behind survival, sustain, pressure, and ability usage. Managing it well is the difference between control and collapse.

**Skill tree progression**
Abilities, passives, modifiers, and upgrades are tied into a progression system that lets the player build around speed, sustain, aggression, mobility, or transformation.

**Forms and power states**
Vampirism is not locked to one shape. Different forms like **Bat Form** and **Ancient Form** open new movement, combat, and utility options.

**Night Hunts**
The hunt system creates dynamic prey events with blood omens, trails, route markers, summoned targets, and rewards. It turns the night into a progression loop instead of just a backdrop.

**Sunburn and daylight weakness**
Daytime is a constant threat. Sunburn escalates, builds pressure, and forces real decisions about timing, exposure, and escape.

**HUD and visual feedback**
Custom icons, hunt markers, screen effects, and blood-focused UI give the mod its own visual language and reinforce the fantasy at every step.

## What defines the mod

- **Infection-first progression** instead of instant vampirism
- **Blood as a core resource** for survival, healing, and abilities
- **Skill tree builds** with passives, modifiers, and specialization
- **Night Hunts** with dynamic prey, routes, and rewards
- **Vampiric forms** with different strengths and playstyles
- **Real daylight pressure** through escalating sunburn
- **Custom presentation** with unique HUD, effects, and hunt visuals

## Cover description

Vampirism is a progression-driven Hytale overhaul where infection leads to ascension, blood fuels power, night hunts drive progression, and the sun is always waiting to burn the unprepared.
